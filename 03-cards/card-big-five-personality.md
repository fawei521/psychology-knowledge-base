# Big Five Personality Model

## 定义

A widely accepted trait model describing personality along five dimensions: Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism (OCEAN).

## 适用条件

- Best for describing stable individual differences.
- Useful for predicting behavior across situations.

## 反例/争议

- Does not explain situational variability.
- Cross-cultural validity varies.
