# James-Lange Theory of Emotion

## 定义

Emotional experience arises from the perception of physiological changes in the body.

## 适用条件

- Explains why bodily feedback influences felt emotion.
- Relevant to anxiety, panic, and somatic symptoms.

## 反例/争议

- Cannot fully explain emotions with minimal physiological differentiation.
- Later theories add cognitive appraisal.
