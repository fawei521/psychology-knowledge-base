# Fundamental Attribution Error

## 定义

The tendency to overestimate dispositional factors and underestimate situational factors when explaining others' behavior.

## 适用条件

- Most pronounced in individualistic cultures.
- Occurs when explaining other people's behavior.

## 反例/争议

- Less pronounced in collectivist cultures.
- People often correct for situational knowledge when explicitly prompted.
