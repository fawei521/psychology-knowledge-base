# Psychology

## 定义

The scientific study of the mind and behavior.

## 适用条件

- Applies to observable and measurable phenomena.
- Uses empirical methods rather than pure logic or authority.

## 反例/争议

- Explanations based on unmeasurable constructs (e.g., "the bird sings because it is happy") are not scientific hypotheses.
