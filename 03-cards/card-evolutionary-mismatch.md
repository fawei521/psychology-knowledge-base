# Evolutionary Mismatch

## 定义

A situation in which traits that were adaptive in ancestral environments become maladaptive in modern environments.

## 适用条件

- Explains overeating, obesity, anxiety about social evaluation, and addiction.
- Useful for understanding why seemingly irrational behaviors persist.

## 反例/争议

- Not all modern problems are mismatches.
- Cultural and individual differences matter.
