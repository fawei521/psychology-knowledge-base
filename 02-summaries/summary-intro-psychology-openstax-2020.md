# OpenStax Psychology 2e - Chapter 1 Summary

## 研究问题

What is psychology, how did it develop historically, and what are its contemporary subfields?

## 核心发现

1. Psychology uses the scientific method to study mind and behavior.
2. Wilhelm Wundt established the first psychology lab in 1879.
3. Major historical approaches: structuralism, functionalism, psychoanalysis, Gestalt, behaviorism, humanism, cognitive revolution.
4. Contemporary psychology includes biopsychology, cognitive, developmental, personality, social, I-O, health, clinical, and forensic psychology.

## 适用边界

- Introductory level; lacks depth for specialized analysis.
- Western-centric historical narrative.
