# Evolutionary Psychology and Physiological Instincts

## 研究问题

How do evolutionary pressures shape human motivation and behavior?

## 核心发现

1. Behavior can be analyzed in terms of survival, reproduction, and social relations.
2. Modern environments may mismatch ancestral adaptations.
3. Emotions serve as adaptive signaling systems.

## 适用边界

- Describes population-level tendencies, not individual determinism.
- Must be complemented by neurobiological and social-learning explanations.
