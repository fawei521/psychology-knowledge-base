# Social Behavior Analysis Framework

## 研究问题

How do social situations influence individual behavior?

## 核心发现

1. Attribution theory distinguishes internal vs. external explanations.
2. Classic studies: Sherif's autokinetic effect, Asch's line study, Milgram's obedience experiments.
3. Group phenomena include social facilitation, social loafing, deindividuation, group polarization, and groupthink.

## 适用边界

- Explains average tendencies, not individual exceptions.
- Ethical concerns limit replication of classic obedience studies.
